This was our first animated product visualization project. I chose to do a short ad for a Macbook Air (2019). My moodboard consisted of a couple of closeups and my color palette was a dark gray for the background, the typical space gray Apple color and a blue, pink and purple image for the screen.

The model I downloaded from Grabcad but made a few adjustments in Fusion 360 and tessellated it. The metal material I made in Substance Designer and the rest I took from Blender Kit. The keyboard symbols I got as a PNG from the downloaded model and just had to edit the Uv-mapping for it to line up. I made a motion preview and worked with an iterative workflow throughout the whole project.
