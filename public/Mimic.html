Mimic Picture

This was a school project, and the assignment was to mimic a real or hyper realistic picture. The model I made using Fusion360 and did some touch ups in Blender. Then I textured and lighted the scene also in Blender to then do post production in Photoshop. (The first image is my 3d made picture, and the second one is the original image.)

This was a fun and very challenging project because I got to choose my own picture and therefore decide my own degree of difficulty. But I only had 4 days to complete the assignment.
